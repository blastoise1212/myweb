All the games in the pokemon franchise are good. The first reason is that there are so many characters. In total there are 932, with some debate on being even higher. Secondly, type advantages are a huge part. There are 18 different types that can have any number of combinations, giving each pokemon strengths and weaknesses, giving the game a unique strategy. Items are a huge part of the game. They can give you the edge over a more powerful opponent, or be what loses the match, so a set to match the pokemon is essential. Moves are one of the biggest parts. They can differ from being status effects hindering the opponent, or sheer base power to destroy your foes. Stats are also very important. Almost everything you do to a pokemon should complement their stats. If you want a meat shield, pump up defence and sp defence, or speed to always go first, or for attack power attack and sp attack. Shinies are also a very interesting mechanic. These pokemon have a different color scheme and are super rare. This means they are highly sought after, and would impress a lot of your friends.
