A look at Minecraft

Made by a very talented builder

Minecraft is a very good game. First, the characters are really customizable. There are probably half a dozen skin editor apps to install. Secondly, there is an open plot where you make your own story, and choose how to play. Thirdly, There are many modes, which affect the intensity and determine if you struggle to survive, or freely build with infinite resources. Building is also one of the biggest parts of minecraft.
