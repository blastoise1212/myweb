My favorite character to play

My favorite Smash player

Super smash bros is one of the greatest games ever. The characters are all very fun to play and unique. For each character a combination of moves, weight, speed, and jump all contribute to a unique fighter, and way to play. The characters in the game are from all over Nintendo’s massive history of game making. There’s metroid, pokemon, mario, and so much more. There is also play styles that need to be mentioned. Some people love to rush into the action and smash some heads. Others like to use the opponents moves against them, and are more patient. Then there is people who love to throw tons of projectiles. Also many of the characters are suited for a style of play, like bowser for brawling, greninja for the counters, and young link to pepper opponents with projectiles. Campaign mode is probably one of the most fun ways to play. In it you battle evil versions of the cast of SSB. you also unlock spirits of various other nintendo characters to power you up, and give you special abilities. Amiibos are also a very fun way to play. Not only can they play idle for you, but they start to learn from battling people, and learn how counter them. Also at a certain level, these amiibos are almost impossible to beat by even the person who trained it. Tournaments are amazing in Smash. They are competitive, diverse, and a lot of fun. Also, this means you don’t need someone in the room to play with them. In SSB there is something for everyone and a massive fan base to prove it.
